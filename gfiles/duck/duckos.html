DuckOS Notice

This message is from the owner of Skyhax. This update will be the last of DuckOS v6, and DuckOS v7's development is still to be decided wether if it will be started or not. Over the past 2 years, the Skyhax community really went through so many changes, some bad and some good. When DuckOS first started, everyone loved it. It was an innovative new design of an operating system, gaining so many positive reviews, and it even being the best rated community operating system in TitaniumNetwork! But as DuckOS grew older and older, the innovations stopped. Skyhax's first and second Discord server got banned, which made the community and website traffic take a huge hit. Difficulty getting new users after this also contributed to the issue. I've lost sight of the goal, of DuckOS being rich in custom content, repeating designs of other operating systems (mainly taking design styles from Apple's MacOS lol). Therefore, I've made the difficult decision to shut down DuckOS v6 once and for all. Our domains and source code will be put up on sale, and hopefully the new owner of Skyhax/DuckOS takes good care of this masterpeice. If, and most likely not, DuckOS v7 continue, it will be under a new name entirely and I will start the community up from scratch. Thank you so much for reading this notice, and I hope you will find other alternatives to DuckOS out there. Take care!
